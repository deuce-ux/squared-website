What is Squared, Really?
Squared is a productivity community for young people who want to do better. Simple as that.
We're not here to pretend we have our lives figured out. We don't. But we know that showing up consistently, fighting procrastination, and having people around you who actually care? That changes everything.
The main idea: You can be productive without pressure. You can have fun AND get things done. You can show up with people who get it.
This is for young people, by young people.
If that sounds like what you need, you're in the right place.
How Squared Actually Works
Alright, let's get into it. Here's how we operate:
🎯 Weekly Goals (The Core System)
Every week, you set 1-2 goals on the Squared app. That's it. Maximum 2.
Not 10 goals. Not a whole vision board. Just the 1 or 2 most important things that would make your week a win.
Examples:
* "Finish my presentation for work"
* "Study 3 hours for my exam"
* "Apply to 5 jobs"
* "Write 2 blog posts"
You set your goals at the start of the week (Monday). Throughout the week, your squad will check in on you:
"how's that presentation going?""Did you hit your study hours?""Need help with anything?"
If you complete your goals mid-week? Add more. But start with 1-2. Focus is everything.
👥 Squads (Your Accountability Squad)
When you join Squared, you're assigned to a Squad — a group of 5 people.
Each Squad has a Squad Leader who keeps the vibe going, checks in on people, and makes sure nobody's ghosting.
Why Squads?
Because 25 people in one big group is chaos. But 5 people in a tight squad? That's family. That's where real accountability happens.
Your Squad becomes your core crew. These are the people who will:
* Check in on your goals
* Celebrate your wins
* Call you out (nicely) when you're slacking
* Be there when things get hard
You're not just in Squared. You're in a Squad. And your Squad has your back.
🧘 Focus Sessions (Work in Silence Together)
This is where the magic happens.
What are focus sessions?
We hop on Jitsi Meet and work in silence together. No talking, just everyone locked in on their work.
Schedule:
* Monday: 8:00pm - 9:30pm
* Thursday: 8:00pm - 9:30pm
* Friday: 8:00pm - 9:30pm
How it works:
1. Join the call (permanent link always available on the app)
2. Work in silence for 30 minutes
3. Chat for 5 minutes
4. Work in silence for 30 minutes
5. Chat for 5 minutes
6. Work in silence for 20 minutes
7. Quick wrap-up, see you next time
Total: 90 minutes of focused work.
Why does this work?
Because working alone is hard. But when you see 10-15 people on a call, all heads down, all grinding? You don't want to be the one scrolling Instagram. The energy is contagious.
🎤 Who Hosts Focus Sessions?
Each Squad rotates hosting duties for one week at a time.
Example rotation:
* Week 1: Squad 1 hosts
* Week 2: Squad 2 hosts
* Week 3: Squad 3 hosts
* And so on…
Within your Squad, you decide who hosts which day. Hosting responsibilities:
* Show up 5 mins early
* Keep time (start/stop work blocks and chat breaks)
* Create good energy
* Post a quick recap after
Why rotation? Because ownership builds community. When it's your week to host, you show up differently.
🏆 Squad Rankings (Monthly Competition)
At the end of every month, we rank Squads based on:
1. Hosting Quality (40%)
2. Attendance Rate (30%)
3. Goal Completion Rate (30%)
Top Squad gets:
* Public shoutout and bragging rights
* Feature on our social media
* Knowing you're that crew 💪
📢 Market Square Sessions
Once a month (last Sunday of the month), we host Market Square Sessions — a live X Space where we bring in guests, share wins, and connect as a community.
Think of it as: Focus sessions = work. Market Square = connection and inspiration.
🏆 Member of the Month
Every month, we recognize one outstanding member with:
* ₦10,000 cash
* Feature on Squared social media
* "Member of the Month" badge for your socials
* First access to special events and opportunities
Eligibility:
* Attended at least 6 focus sessions that month
* Completed at least 75% of weekly goals
* Actively engaged in the community
* Current on membership payment
The prize is only awarded if at least 70% of the square has made payment. When everyone shows up, everyone benefits.
📚 Learning & Resource Sharing
Someone found a great productivity app? Share it. Someone learned a new skill and wants to teach others? Host a mini-session.
Squared isn't just accountability. It's a space to learn from each other.
The Vibe: What Squared IS and ISN'T

* Accountability without judgment
* Fun, informal, and real
* A community that actually cares

Community Guidelines

* Show up. Consistency matters more than perfection.
* Support others. Hype people up. Celebrate wins.
* Be honest. If you're falling behind, say so. We'll help.
* Engage. Don't just lurk.
* Respect everyone's journey.
* Pay on time. By the 5th of each month.
❌ Don't Do This:
* Ghost. If you'll be inactive, let us know.
* Be negative or toxic.
* Spam.
* Make excuses forever.
Mandatory Requirements & Consequences
1. Focus Session Attendance
Minimum: 4 sessions per month
* First month below 4: We check in with you.
* Second month below 4: Final warning.
* Third consecutive month: We'll have to remove you.
Life happens — just communicate with your Squad Leader or Covenant in advance.
2. Weekly Goals & Activity
Minimum: Post goals at least 3 out of 4 weeks per month
* Week 1 of silence: Squad Leader DMs you.
* Week 2 of silence: Covenant reaches out personally.
* No response by end of week 2: We'll assume you're no longer interested and free up your spot.
3. Payment
₦4,000/month keeps everything running. If you're going to be late, just reach out. Communication is everything. Don't go silent.
4. Community Standards
Zero tolerance for disrespect, spam, or harassment. One warning. If it continues, you're out.
Grace & Communication Policy
If you're going through something — just tell us. Message your Squad Leader or Covenant:
"Hey, I'm dealing with [situation] for the next 2 weeks. I'll be less active but I'm still committed."
We'll work with you. But ghosting without communication isn't okay.
Roles at Squared
Squared isn't just a community you attend — it's one you help build. As we enter Squared 3.0, we're opening up leadership roles that let members take real ownership of how Squared runs and grows.
These roles exist because we believe the best communities are built by the people inside them. Every role here is an opportunity to learn by doing, build something real, and grow in ways that matter beyond Squared.
🎖️ Squad Leader
(4-5 per Square)
Squad Leaders are the heartbeat of Squared. Every squad of 5 members has a leader whose job is simple — make sure nobody gets left behind.
What they do:
* Lead a squad of 5 members
* Facilitate Tuesday and Thursday check-ins
* Make sure everyone sets their goals and posts wrap-ups on the app
* Support squad members who are struggling
* Report issues to the Square Lead
Time commitment: 2-3 hours/week
Why this role exists: Systems don't keep people accountable — people do. Squad Leaders are the human layer that makes everything else work.
Who should apply: You've been in Squared for 2+ months, you show up consistently, you're a good listener, and you genuinely enjoy seeing other people win.
🤝 Support Board
(3-5 people, serves all Squares)
The Support Board exists because we don't just want to drop links and resources on people — we want to actually help them move forward.
What they do:
* Find and curate weekly opportunities — jobs, hackathons, grants, events
* Help members actually apply — review CVs, give feedback on portfolios
* Handle support requests around accountability, mental health, and life challenges
* Connect struggling members with resources or people who can help
Time commitment: 2-3 hours/week
Why this role exists: Information is everywhere. What most people lack is someone who will sit with them and help them act on it. That's what this team does.
Who should apply: You're empathetic, resourceful, patient, and good at research. You're the person people naturally come to when they need help figuring things out.
📢 Brand & Media
(2-3 people, serves all Squares)
Brand & Media is responsible for how Squared shows up to the world and how alive it feels on the inside. This isn't a design role — it's a storytelling and culture role.
What they do:
* Generate post ideas for Squared's social media (you're not designing or posting — just ideating and writing)
* Interview members for Weekly Win Spotlights
* Invite and interview external guests — founders, creators, experts
* Organize squad games and social activities
* Keep community energy high and culture strong
* Everything you write, you're credited for
Time commitment: 1-2 hours/week
Why this role exists: A community without culture is just a group chat. Brand & Media makes Squared feel like somewhere worth being.
Who should apply: You're creative, energetic, and a natural storyteller. You love content, you're interested in writing, and you know how to hype people up without it feeling forced.
⚙️ Operations Lead
(1 per Square)
The Operations Lead keeps everything running behind the scenes so the Square Lead can focus on the bigger picture.
What they do:
* Track payments — who has paid, who is overdue, send reminders
* Coordinate focus session hosting across squads
* Maintain squad rosters and update them as members join or leave
* Handle admin tasks that keep the square organized
Time commitment: 2-3 hours/week
Why this role exists: Great communities don't run themselves. Someone has to make sure the logistics are tight so everything else can flow.
Who should apply: You're organized, detail-oriented, reliable, and you actually enjoy logistics. You're the person who notices when something is off before anyone else does.
Why Take On a Role?
These aren't honorary titles. They're real responsibilities — and real opportunities.
Learn by doing. Every role teaches you something valuable. Operations learns how to run systems. Brand & Media learns content and storytelling. Support Board learns how to coach and connect people. Squad Leaders learn how to lead.
Build your resume. "Squad Leader at Squared" is something you can point to. You did real work, with real people, and real results.
Get recognized. Role badges, public shoutouts, and status within the community. Your contribution won't go unnoticed.
Get access first. Role holders are the first to know about opportunities, partnerships, and what's coming next in Squared.
Own it. This stops being something you attend and starts being something that's yours.
How to Apply for a Role
If any of these roles resonate with you, we want to hear from you.
To apply, send Covenant a DM with:
* The role you're interested in
* Why you want it (be honest, not formal)
* One thing that makes you a good fit
No long forms, no interviews. Just a real conversation.
A few things to note: roles are assigned based on fit, consistency, and genuinely wanting to contribute. If a role goes unfilled and no one steps up, Covenant reserves the right to tap someone directly — so if you've been thinking about it, don't wait. Volunteer before you get voluntold. 😅
Roles are reviewed every 3 months. If it's not working out, we'll figure it out together — no hard feelings.
This is your chance to go from member to builder. Don't sleep on it. 🔥

FAQ
"What if I can't make a focus session?" No stress. Life happens. Just show up when you can and aim for at least 4 a month.
"What if I don't hit my goals?" That's okay. Drop what happened in your squad chat. We'll figure out what went wrong and adjust.
"Can I change my goals mid-week?" Yes. If you finish early, add more. If something urgent comes up, adjust.
"What if my Squad isn't vibing?" Talk to Covenant directly. Squads should feel supportive, not stressful.
"How do I cancel or pause my membership?" Message Covenant directly. Just don't ghost — that's not cool.
"Who do I contact if I have questions?" Covenant. DM on WhatsApp anytime.
Final Thoughts

It's not going to fix your life overnight. It's not going to make procrastination disappear.
