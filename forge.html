What is Forge

Not a course.
A proving ground.

Most programs teach you things. Forge makes you do things. Every session has a theme, a task, and an expectation. You show up, you learn, and you leave with something made.

It's intense because making things is intense. But you're not doing it alone — your cohort is right there with you, struggling and shipping and figuring it out together.

By the end of each edition, you'll have a body of work. Real things you made. Not just knowledge in your head — proof you can do the thing.

You Ship Things
Every session ends with something made. A project, a piece, a product — something you can point at and say "I made this." Knowledge without output doesn't count.
Small Cohort
No massive classes. Each edition runs with a small group — everyone knows each other, everyone holds each other accountable. The size is intentional.
One Skill at a Time
Each edition focuses on exactly one skill and goes deep on it. Not a taster course. Not an overview. You leave knowing how to actually do the thing.
Forge 1.0 · Brand Identity
