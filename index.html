The Problem

People are not lazy. They are just doing hard things completely alone.

97%
of people who enroll in online courses never finish them. Information is not the gap.
92%
of people who set goals never achieve them. Most quit before February ends.
95%
more likely to achieve a goal when you have a specific accountability appointment with someone.
40%+
youth unemployment in Nigeria. Young people building skills with zero structure around them.

The problem was never discipline or information. It was isolation.

Squared exists to solve exactly that — by making consistency a shared, social, and supported experience.

What Squared Is

An OS for the ambitious young Nigerian.

Think of Squared as the operating system running in the background of a young person's life. It gives them structure without rigidity, accountability without pressure, and community without noise. Members don't just consume content — they show up, set goals, and build something real alongside people who genuinely care.

We have built squads, focus sessions, role-based leadership, support systems, and a culture that keeps people coming back month after month. Squared is where ambition becomes action.

🎯
Weekly Goal System
Members set 1–2 focused weekly goals on the Squared app. Tracked, visible, and followed up on by squad leaders.
👥
Squad Accountability
Every member belongs to a squad of 5 with a dedicated leader who checks in twice weekly and notices when you go quiet.
🧘
Focus Sessions
3x weekly silent co-working sessions. Mon, Thu, Fri at 8PM. 90 minutes of collective, focused work.
🤝
Support Board
A dedicated team that reviews CVs, shares opportunities, and holds members' hands through real challenges.
