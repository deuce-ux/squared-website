BEFORE YOU CONTINUE

Here's what membership looks like.

Squared is not a passive community. If you're in, you're expected to show up. Here's the minimum:

  • Attend at least 4 focus sessions out of every 12 scheduled
  • Participate actively in your squad — check in, respond, show up for your people
  • Attend at least 1 Market Square session every 2 months
  • Set your weekly goals every Monday
  • Communicate if you're going through something — we work with life, not against it

These aren't rules to police you. They're the minimum for the community to actually work.
