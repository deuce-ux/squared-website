Guess what we can do

We’re a bunch of creatives who love blending art and tech to build experiences that actually make people feel something. From smooth animations to bold digital experiences, we bring ideas to life. Our goal? To dive into exciting projects, team up with amazing collaborators, and deliver top-tier services that Web3 communities around the world can vibe with.

Web & UI/UX Design

Bean Studio crafts sleek, user-first web3 experiences that not only look incredible but feel seamless — perfect for dApps, NFT drops, and everything in between.

Animation & Visual Art

From eye-catching NFT animations to vibrant visuals for your metaverse brand, our art squad brings your digital identity to life — with style and flair.

Automation & Bot Building

Whether it’s Discord bots or task automation across web3 tools, we build smart, snappy bots that make your life easier and your community smoother.

Content Strategy

From Twitter threads to onboarding guides, we create content that educates, entertains, and brings your community into the web3 loop — the Bean way.

Discord & Community Building

We help DAOs and projects grow vibrant, engaging Discord hubs with roles, bots, vibes, and structure that keep your members hyped and connected.

Partnerships & Collabs

We’ve only worked on a few small projects so far, mostly within grassroots web3 communities. Still, every collaboration has taught us something valuable and strengthened our approach. We’re new, but we’re hungry to learn and excited for what’s next.
